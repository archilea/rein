# Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant, version 2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in Rein a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour can be reported by emailing `conduct@archilea.com`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

Read the full Contributor Covenant 2.1 linked above for the complete standards and enforcement guidelines.
